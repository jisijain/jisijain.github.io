How we'll work

The flow

Every engagement is structured yet flexible: chill, but strategic. Here's the shape of it.

  1. 01

    Discover

    Goals, constraints and users, aligned before anything is designed.

  2. 02

    Define

    Research synthesised into a sharp problem and clear success metrics.

  3. 03

    Design

    Wireframes to high-fi UI, built on a real, reusable system.

  4. 04

    Validate

    Prototype, test with users, iterate until it genuinely works.

  5. 05

    Deliver

    Specs, tokens and API contracts for rework-free engineering.

  6. 06

    Refine

    Measure, learn and optimise once it's live in the world.
